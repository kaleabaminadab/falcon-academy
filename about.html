Our Mission and History

The Falcon Walker Vision

Our Mission: To inspire students to pursue intellectual curiosity, demonstrate ethical leadership, and achieve excellence in a global society.

Our Vision: To be recognized as a leading academy dedicated to innovative teaching and holistic student development.

A Legacy of Learning

Falcon Walker Academy was founded in 1995 by Dr. Eleanor Vance with the belief that a balanced education—combining rigorous academics with strong moral principles—is the key to producing successful future leaders. For over two decades, we have maintained a commitment to small class sizes and personalized mentorship.
