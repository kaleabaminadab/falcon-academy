We are excited you are considering Falcon Walker Academy. Our application process is designed to ensure a mutual fit between the student and our values.

The 4-Step Admissions Process

1. Inquiry & Tour

Start by filling out our brief inquiry form and scheduling a private campus tour to see our learning environment firsthand.

Schedule a Visit

2. Complete Application

Submit the online application form, including previous academic records, required essays, and teacher recommendations by the deadline.

Start Application

3. Student Assessment

Applicants will be invited for a standardized assessment and a short interview with a member of our admissions committee.

4. Decision & Enrollment

Receive your admission decision. Upon acceptance, secure your place by submitting the enrollment contract and initial deposit.
