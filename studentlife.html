The Falcon Walker Academic Program

Our curriculum is designed to challenge students intellectually while fostering creativity and independent thought across all grade levels.

Academic Philosophy

We utilize a project-based learning (PBL) methodology, which encourages students to tackle real-world problems. This hands-on approach ensures not only knowledge acquisition but also mastery of collaboration, research, and presentation skills.

Core Programs Overview

Lower School (Grades K-5)

  • Foundational Literacy & Numeracy
  • Introduction to Coding
  • Integrated Arts and Movement

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

  • Pre-AP Science & Math
  • Global Studies & Languages
  • Digital Citizenship and Media Literacy

Upper School (Grades 9-12)

  • Advanced Placement (AP) Courses
  • College Counseling & Portfolio Development
  • Capstone Research Project
